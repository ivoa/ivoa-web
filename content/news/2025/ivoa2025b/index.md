The IVOA November 2025 Interoperability will be held by DZA (Deutches
Zentrum für Astronomie) in Görlitz (Germany) on 14-16 November 2025.

The [meeting website](https://indico.ict.inaf.it/event/3325), with 
further details, and the 
[detailed schedule](https://wiki.ivoa.net/twiki/bin/view/IVOA/InterOpNov2025)
are already available.

The meeting will follow the [ADASS XXXV Conference](https://indico.dzastro.de/event/4), held at Görlitz
as well.
